Some lines are too small to be anything but kept. Here are three.

<div class="poem">
  <p>
    The garden does not ask<br>
    who planted it.<br>
    It simply does the long, green work<br>
    of becoming itself.
  </p>
</div>

<hr class="ornament" />

<div class="poem">
  <p>
    Tea cools in the cup<br>
    while we argue about the moon &mdash;<br>
    and the moon, indifferent,<br>
    keeps its own counsel.
  </p>
</div>

<hr class="ornament" />

<div class="poem">
  <p>
    Of all the doors I have stood before,<br>
    the ones I remember<br>
    are the ones that were already open,<br>
    and I walked past.
  </p>
</div>

<p class="poem-note">
  <em>Loose translations &mdash; the kind made for keeping, not for scholars.</em>
</p>
