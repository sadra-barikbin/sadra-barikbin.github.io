Some afternoons don&rsquo;t give you a story, only a palette. This was one of those.
I wasn&rsquo;t trying to photograph anything in particular &mdash; just the light, and
what it did to ordinary things.

{% include gallery caption="Three frames, kept for their colour rather than their subject." %}

I don&rsquo;t think any of these are *good* photographs, in the way a photographer
would mean it. But they are honest about the hour they were taken, and that turns
out to be the thing I wanted to keep.
